Where It All Began

Two college friends. One broken laptop. Zero funding. In 2022, frustrated with blogging platforms that prioritised ads over authors, we built Blogify in a Lucknow hostel room. We wanted a place that felt like a library — calm, focused, and built entirely around the written word.

Three years later, we're home to 50,000+ writers across India and beyond — and that hostel room is now a real office. The mission hasn't changed one bit.

The Impact We've Made

Numbers don't tell the whole story — but they hint at it. In three years we've watched students become columnists, engineers become novelists, and shy first-timers become communities of thousands.

And one thing we're quietly proud of? Zero ads. Ever. Your reader's attention belongs to your words — not a banner for a mattress brand.

The People Behind It

We're a small, fiercely passionate team of designers, engineers, and writers who use Blogify ourselves every single day. That's not a marketing line — our entire company blog runs on the same platform you use.
